Grilled Cheese

The grilled cheese is a hot cheese sandwich typically prepared by heating slices of cheese between slices of bread with a cooking fat such as butter or mayonnaise on a frying pan, griddle, or sandwich toaster, until the bread browns and the cheese melts.

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of bread
  • 3 tablespoons softened butter
  • 2 slices of cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat a nonstick skillet over medium heat.
  2. Generously butter both sides of 2 slices of bread
  3. Place one slice down in the hot skillet; add 1 slice of cheese.
  4. Place second slice on top of cheese.
  5. Cook until lightly browned on one side; flip over and continue cooking until cheese is melted. Repeat with remaining 2 slices of bread, butter, and slice of cheese.
